The Overtones showcase a broad range of the most popular big band dance music from Glenn Miller and Contemporary, with stops at Broadway, Las Vegas and the Tonight Show.

Since 1997, The Overtones of Kansas City have been playing the best known big band standards and top contemporary music. Big bands are back and we are ready to entertain you with music that is begging to be danced to.

We've performed for weddings, anniversaries, city festivals, corporate dinners, fund raisers and veteran appreciation events. We can tailor your performance to the dancer in all of us or to the sentimentalist who just wants to bring back memories of a simpler time.

Regardless of your age or musical tastes, if you like Big Band at all you will LOVE the OVERTONES!
