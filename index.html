The Overtones of Kansas City Big Band showcases a broad range of the most popular big band dance music ranging from the Glenn Miller era all the way through Contemporary, with stops at Broadway, Vegas, and Hollywood.

Since 1997, The Overtones of Kansas City have been playing the best known big band standards and top contemporary music. Big bands are back and we are ready to entertain you with music that is begging you to hit the dance floor.

We've performed for weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, city festivals, corporate dinners, fund raisers and veteran appreciation events. We can tailor our performance to fit your entertainment needs.

Regardless of your age or musical tastes, you will LOVE the OVERTONES!
